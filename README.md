# scaling-palm-tree-ganeshdarshan
---
In the current implementation, I studied the dataset to a limited extent and tried to break it down into meaningful patterns. From the conversations, I observed that most user queries fall into a few key categories such as product enquiries, offer-related searches, pricing queries, and order-related complaints. I have analysed the human interactoin with the assistant which helps to understand how the assistant performed and was it as effeective
---
Based on this, I attempted to group the conversations into these buckets to better understand what users are actually searching for and how they interact with the assistant. After categorizing, I focused on analyzing user behaviour — whether users were simply interacting with the assistant, actively looking for products, or engaging with the product recommendations provided.
---
Additionally, I tried to derive some insights around the effectiveness of the AI itself. This includes observing whether the assistant was able to guide users toward products, whether users responded to those suggestions, and overall how efficiently the AI handled different types of queries.
